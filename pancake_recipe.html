Simple Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp of sunflower or vegatable oil
  • syrup to serve (optional)

Method

Step 1

Put the plain flour, large eggs, milk, oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.

Step 2

Set aside for 30 mins to rest. Unless you're super lazy, then you can skip this step.

Step 3

Set a medium sized frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.

Step 4

Set When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.

Step 5

Serve with syrup or any topping your choice. Once cold, you can layer the pancakes between baking parchment, then wrap in cling film and freeze for up to 2 months.
