Simple Pancake Recipe


by Jack Black

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy peasy lemon squeasy

Makes: 12


Nutrition per pancake:
kcl: fat: saturates: carbs:
61 2g 1g 7g
sugars: fibre: protein: salt:
1g 1g 3g 0.1g

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp of sunflower or vegatable oil
  • syrup to serve (optional)

Method Steps

  1. Put the plain flour, large eggs, milk, oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest. Unless you're super lazy, then you can skip this step.
  3. Set a medium sized frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. Set When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  5. Serve with syrup or any topping your choice. Once cold, you can layer the pancakes between baking parchment, then wrap in cling film and freeze for up to 2 months.
